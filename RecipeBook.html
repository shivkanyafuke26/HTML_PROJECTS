Puran Poli

Puran Poli is a traditional Indian sweet flatbread made by stuffing a soft dough with a sweet filling of cooked lentils and jaggery, flavored with cardamom and nutmeg. It's a popular festive treat enjoyed across India.

Ingredients:

  • cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • A pinch of salt
  • Water, as needed

    • For the filling (Puran):
  • 1 cup chana dal (split Bengal gram)
  • 1 cup jaggery (grated)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg powder
  • 1 tablespoon ghee

Instructions:

  1. Cook chana dal until soft. Mash it and mix with jaggery, cardamom powder, nutmeg powder, and ghee. Cook until thickened.
  2. Mix whole wheat flour,oil or ghee,and salt.Knead into a soft dough with water.Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Divide dough and filling into balls.
  4. Roll out dough into a circle, place filling in the center,seal,and roll out into a flatbread.
  5. Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown, applying ghee or oil on both sides.

Khichdi

Khichdi is a classic Indian dish consisting of rice and lentils cooked together. It's a staple comfort food known for its simplicity and nutritional value.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow mung dal (moong dal)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and soak rice and mung dal for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee/oil, add cumin and mustard seeds.
  3. Saut√© onion, ginger-garlic paste, and tomato until soft.
  4. Add turmeric, salt, drained rice, and dal. Stir.
  5. Pour water, cook until soft (3-4 whistles in a pressure cooker).
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
